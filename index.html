About us

Fruit Block - Puzzle Legend is an addictive match-3 puzzle game where players combine colorful fruits to clear levels and complete challenges.

With hundreds of levels, exciting power-ups, and special events, Fruit Block offers endless fun for players of all ages.

The gameplay is simple but increasingly challenging, perfect for those who love casual puzzle games with a strategic twist.
