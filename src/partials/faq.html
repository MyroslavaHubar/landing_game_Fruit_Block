FAQ

How do I start playing
Fruit Block - Puzzle
Legend?

Download from the Play
Store and tap 'Play'
to begin.

Is the game free to play?

Yes, but it offers optional
in-app purchases for
boosters and special items.

Can I play without an
internet connection?

Yes, the game supports
offline play.

What are boosters, and
how do I get them?

Boosters help you
clear tough levels
and can be earned
by completing challenges
or purchased in-game.

How often are new levels
added?

New levels and challenges
are added regularly to keep
the game fresh and exciting.

Can I compete with
friends?

Yes, connect through
social media to see
your friends' progress
and compete for high scores.

What are the seasonal
events?

Seasonal events offer
special themed levels
and unique rewards
for limited periods of time.

How can I earn more
stars?

Complete levels with
fewer moves to earn
more stars and unlock
additional rewards.

Can I sync my progress
across devices?

Yes, connect to your
social media account
to save and sync your game progress on multiple devices.

Are there daily
challenges?

Yes, complete daily tasks
to earn rewards like coins,
boosters, and more!
